Ufology in Canada: A History of the Unknown

For decades, Canadians have reported strange lights in the sky, unidentified craft, and unexplained encounters. Some believe these are misidentified aircraft, natural phenomena, or hoaxes. Others argue they are evidence of something beyond our understanding.

But UFO sightings aren’t just isolated stories—they form patterns over time. When we examine them as data, we begin to see something unexpected.
